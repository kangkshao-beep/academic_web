I am currently a visitor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST).

My current work focuses on inclusive charm decays, with particular interests in Heavy Quark Effective Theory (HQET), Operator Product Expansion (OPE), and heavy-flavor phenomenology. I am especially interested in the interface between analytic theory, data processing, and data-driven extraction of non-perturbative parameters from experimental observables.

Recently, I have been working on decay widths and electron-energy moments in inclusive charm decays, infrared-end extrapolation of measured electron spectra, and phenomenological analyses based on CLEO and BESIII inputs. This site collects my CV, current research directions, journal publications, conference reports, open questions, and a small photography selection.
